  • Effective Communication
    1. Media used for communication should be the most appropriate to achieve the aim of communication.
    2. Physical proximity (nearness) makes communication more effective.
    3. The principle of 'need to know' should be kept in mind.
    4. Knowledge, experiance and maturity of the receiver of information must be understood by the sender.
    5. What is to be achieved by a particular communication?
    6. Written message must be clear and concise.
    7. Keep the communication as simple as possible.

  • Characteristics of a Good Speech or Presentation
    1. Clarity about what is to be spoken
    2. Command over language used
    3. Not too long, not too short
    4. Say something new
    5. Appeal to head and heart
    6. must suit the background and experience of the audience
    7. The speech must not be too formal
    8. Solid information through concrete facts
    9. Never be artificial and copy another's style
